What is Cyberbullying ?

Cyberbullying is bullying that takes place over digital devices like cell phones, computers, and tablets. Cyberbullying can occur through SMS, Text, and apps, or online in social media, forums, or gaming where people can view, participate in, or share content. It is repeated behaviours, aimed at scaring, angering or shaming those who are targeted.

The most common places where cyberbullying occurs

Social Media, Text messaging, Online chatting, Online forums, chat rooms, Email, Online gaming communities.
